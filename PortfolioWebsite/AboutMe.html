I'm Gerben Prins working towards becoming a game developer. I live in the Netherlands close to Amsterdam where I right now study game development/programming. Video Games have always caught my attention and I've always wanted to be able to make them. I am pretty much interested in every aspect of Video games but am mostly Skilled With unity and C#.

When it comes to games: I’m mostly interested in platformers and usually play precision platformers like Celeste or Mario kaizo/ Rom hacks because I like the challenge but a lot of the times I like to relax as well and play something less complicated.

Don’t worry though I would love to develop other things as well and expand my knowledge even further until I can gladly tell someone to play a game I worked on for years!
