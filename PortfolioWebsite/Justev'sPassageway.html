Justev's Passageway

Justevâ€™s passageway is a tiny project I worked on to experiment with pathfinding for enemies. I eventually made a little game for it where the player would have to find a key and bring it to the top of the map without being spotted by any of the enemies.

Path Finding

The enemies are coded with a pathfinding script that navigates to a random location on the map. Once they reach this point they go to another place and they repeat this process infinitely. Once the enemy spots the player they stop following the waypoints and chase the player instead.
